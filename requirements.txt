User requirments:
There will be an airplane that will move up, down, left, right by the arrow keys. The airplane will travel through buildings (terrain). User will get 1 point for every 1/10 of a second they survive. If the airplane hits the a building then the game will end, game over will be printed.

Buildings will move from right to left on the screen. Buildings will be randomly generated but coherent. It will be challenging but not impossible to move through.
